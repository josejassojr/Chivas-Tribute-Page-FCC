Who are "Las Chivas"?

Las Chivas are a Mexican soccer club founded in 1906 in the city of Guadalajara in the state of Jalisco, Mexico. It is the most popular club in all of Mexico due in part to their tradition of only playing Mexican players on their team. They are the only team in Mexico to do so and have had great success throughout their history, being one of the most decorated teams in all of Latin America. Their iconic red and white striped shirts were adopted from Belgian club Club Brugge at the time of their founding. They have contributed many great players to the national team and players who have gone on to play in big clubs in Europe as well including: Omar Bravo, Javier Hernandez, Francisco Rodriguez, Carlos Salcido, and Carlos Vela amongst many others.
